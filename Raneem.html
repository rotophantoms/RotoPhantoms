Team Member - Saja

Role: Programmer

Saja Khalid Al-Qasabi is a programmer in the Roto Phantoms team with a passion for programming, robotics, and creative design. She enjoys video editing, filming content, and designing posts using Canva. Saja also loves participating in competitions, school events, and school forums, as well as working in teams and taking on leadership roles. She is passionate about combining technology with creativity and is confident in public speaking and presentations. Saja participated in the Student Innovations Competition during 2025–2026. She has also attended several workshops, including the Digital Lab Creative Coding Course at Sultan Qaboos University. In addition, she participated in the Alqundus Olympiad, along with several school-level mathematics competitions and activities. With her team, Saja contributed to organizing and delivering educational workshops to benefit students and the community. These included a Vinci Bot robotics workshop for children at Al Mashariq School and a VEX IQ workshop at her school to introduce students to the competition and train them. One of Saja’s future goals is to further develop her skills in programming and robotics, and to share the knowledge she has gained with others to contribute to technological development and serve the community.
