Team Member - Jood

Role: Team Leader

Jood Qasim Awlad Thani is a builder, leader, and former programmer in the Roto Phantoms team with a passion for robotics, engineering, and innovation. Her robotics journey began at the age of seven when she participated in the FIRST LEGO League Jr. international competition in Jordan, where her team won first place and the Most Creative Programming Award. Over the years, Jood has participated in several robotics competitions, including the FIRST Global Challenge qualification competitions in Oman, where she helped lead her team to second place nationally, as well as the VEX IQ Competition, where her team achieved the highest driving skills score in Al Dakhiliyah and won awards for Best Engineering Notebook and Best Robot Design. She enjoys building and driving robots, coding in languages such as Python, Java, and HTML, and taking on leadership and public speaking roles. Jood has also attended and presented workshops in robotics, coding, and 3D printing, helping train younger students and teams in platforms such as VEX IQ and EV3. As part of an all-girls team, one of her biggest goals is to inspire more girls to pursue STEM and to one day represent Oman internationally.
