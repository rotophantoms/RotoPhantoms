Team Member - Sheyam

Role: Designer & Content Creator

Sheyam Sultan Albusaidi has been passionate about robotics and innovation since 2022. Throughout their journey, they have participated in several competitions related to technology, engineering, and creativity. They achieved second place in the Sultanate of Oman in a design competition focused on creating the best futuristic Minecraft city. They also participated in an innovation competition, where they reached the semifinals in Oman by developing biofuel from the lote tree. In addition, Sheyam took part in the VEX IQ Challenge as the team's builder, helping lead the team to victory through teamwork and problem-solving skills. Sheyam is experienced in programming languages and platforms such as Python, Micro:bit, HTML, Arduino, Visual Studio Code, and VEX IQ programming. Alongside programming, they have strong building and design experience developed through workshops in 3D printing, construction, and engineering design. They continuously strive to improve their skills in robotics, innovation, and technology.
